About Us

Our Story: Roots by the River

Naturally Bristol began with a simple belief: that eating well shouldn’t be a luxury, and plant-based food should never be boring. Our founder is a lifelong Bristolian and a dedicated "foodie" who spent years exploring the city's famous food markets and independent stalls. Driven by a passion for the vibrant, healthy potential of local ingredients, they set out to create a space where everyone—from lifelong vegans to curious meat-eaters—could enjoy a truly delicious meal without breaking the bank.

A Bright Space in the Heart of the Harbour

Located just a stone's throw from the iconic S.S. Great Britain and overlooking the tranquil waters of the Floating Harbour, our restaurant is designed to be your riverside sanctuary. We’ve traded dark corners for double-height windows and "warm minimalist" decor, creating a bright and airy environment where the outside world feels just a glance away.You'll see our philosophy reflected in our logo—the fig leaves and vines wrapping around the letters NB. It represents our commitment to growth, nature, and the local Bristol community. Whether you’re stopping in for a quick breakfast before a walk across the Clifton Suspension Bridge or settling in for a long dinner as the sun sets over the river, we invite you to pull up a chair.At Naturally Bristol, we don’t just serve food; we celebrate the flavours of our city, one local harvest at a time. Welcome to the family.
